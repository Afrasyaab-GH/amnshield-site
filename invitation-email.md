# AmnShield Tester Invitation Email Template

Copy and send this email template to your beta testers after you add their email address to the Google Play Console Closed Testing track.

---

```markdown
Subject: Welcome to AmnShield Closed Beta Testing! 🚀

Assalamu Alaikum,

Alhamdulillah, your email has been authorized and added to the AmnShield closed beta testing group! 

Please follow these quick steps on your Android device to install the app:

1. Click this link to join the testing group:
   👉 https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.alhaq.deenshield

2. Important: Make sure you are signed in on your browser with the same Google Account/Gmail you submitted in the form.

3. Click the blue "BECOME A TESTER" button on that page.

4. Once done, click "Download it on Google Play" to download and install AmnShield directly from the Play Store!

If you encounter any issues downloading, or want to send us feedback/bugs during testing, please reply directly to this email.

Jazakumullahu Khairan for helping us test and improve AmnShield for the Ummah!

Best regards,
The AmnShield Team
```
