We're here to help

AmnShield is a free tool built and maintained by Al-Haq Studio and offered through the Al-Haq Initiative. Below are the best ways to get help or share feedback.

Get in Touch

For bugs, feature requests, or help setting up the app, please email us. As a small founder-led project, we read every message and aim to respond within 24–48 hours.

  • General Enquiries: info@alhaq.uk
  • Technical Support: support@alhaq.uk

Reporting an Issue

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include the following in your email:

  • Your device model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S23)
  • Your Android version (e.g., Android 14)
  • A clear description of what happened and what you expected to happen.
  • Exported Logs: Go to Settings → Error Reporting → Export Report and attach the file to your email.

Community & Feedback

AmnShield is built for the Ummah — your feedback directly shapes what gets built next. If you are on the closed Beta testing track via Google Play, please also leave a rating through the Play Store console.
