Features

Explore the App

1

Home Dashboard

Your protection at a glance

The Home screen shows your current protection status — how many features are active, today's screen time, reels scrolled, and your daily goal progress. Tap any feature chip to jump straight to its configuration.

shield Protection status updates in real time
2

Blocks & Rules

Your protection control centre

Toggle App Blocker, Keyword Filter, Website Blocker, and Focus Mode with a single tap. Under Advanced Rules, set up cheat hours for controlled access windows, recurring block schedules, and per-app launch limits.

lock Core Protection & Advanced Rules in one place
3

Keyword Filter

Real-time on-screen detection

Add words to your blocklist — when any keyword appears on-screen in any app, AmnShield instantly blocks the view or navigates back. Supports wildcards and pre-configured content packs. All detection runs locally via the Accessibility Service.

search No keywords ever leave your device
4

Focus Mode

Deep work, no distractions

Start a timed focus session — choose your duration, select which apps to lock down, and let AmnShield handle the rest. Track your earned focus minutes and build a measurable habit over time. Supports auto-focus schedules for recurring sessions.

my_location 15-minute to 2-hour sessions with app selection
5

Usage Stats

Know your habits, change them

See your daily screen time, most-used apps, reels scrolled, and blocking events. Drill into per-app breakdowns with website visit tracking. All data stays on your device — AmnShield has no servers collecting your activity.

bar_chart 100% local data, zero cloud uploads
6

Settings

Make it yours

Choose from five visual themes — Default, Gradient, Emerald, Sunset, or Purple. Enable anti-uninstall protection with password or timed locks. Configure daily report notifications, manage error reporting preferences, and review your privacy settings.

tune 5 themes, anti-uninstall lock, full privacy control
First Launch

Required Permissions

AmnShield needs three Android permissions to protect you. Each one is explained below — we only ask for what we need, and nothing leaves your device.

accessibility_new

Accessibility Service

The backbone of AmnShield. It detects which app is in the foreground and reads on-screen text to enforce keyword blocks — all processing happens on-device.

data_usage

Usage Access

Allows AmnShield to measure your screen time and enforce Launch Limits. Required for the Stats dashboard and daily usage reports.

layers

Display Over Apps

Required to show the block screen or Smart Blur overlay when a restricted app or keyword is detected. Without this, AmnShield can't visually intervene.
