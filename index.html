AmnShield

System-Level Blocker

Your un-bypassable accessibility guard. Intercepts explicit browser URLs against 50k+ local domains, restricts distracting apps, blocks infinite-scrolling social reels, and filters custom keywords in real time. Features scheduled rules and launch limits.

AmnGaze

AI Visual Protector

Your real-time visual filter. Uses local, on-device machine learning (TensorFlow Lite) to scan your screen projection dynamically. Automatically blurs explicit imagery/nudity and enforces a full-screen hard block redirecting you home upon severe violations.
